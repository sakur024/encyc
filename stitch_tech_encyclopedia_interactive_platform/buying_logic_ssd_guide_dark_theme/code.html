Critical Pitfalls

warning

Ignoring the Motherboard Interface

Purchasing a PCIe Gen 5.0 NVMe SSD for a motherboard that only supports PCIe Gen 3.0. The drive will work, but it will be bottlenecked to Gen 3.0 speeds, wasting your investment.

info

Overpaying for Unnoticeable Speeds

For standard gaming and daily tasks, the difference between a high-end PCIe Gen 4.0 drive and an entry-level Gen 3.0 drive is often indistinguishable in real-world use.
